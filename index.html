In the Heart of the Sea

In the Heart of the Sea is a non-fiction book detailing the destruction of the whaleship Essex. It is drawn mainly from two accounts, that of the First Mate, Owen Chase, and that of the ships Cabin Boy, Thomas Nickerson.
